Description

These are two interactive representations: the one on the left is a scatter plot, while the one below is based on parallel coordinates.

On both it is possible to select specific data and see relative details on the table at the bottom of this page.

Moreover in the scatter plot graph it is possible to highlight all dots of a specific category by clicking on the corresponding one on the legend.

You can also perform a cross selection on both representations.

To reset graphs after a selection, just double click into their areas.

It is also possible to obtain a heart failure prediction by clicking here.
